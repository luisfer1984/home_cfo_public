Privacy Policy

Home CFO

Overview

Home CFO is a personal finance and life management tool. If you connect third-party services (e.g., health providers), Home CFO uses that data only to provide insights inside the app.

Data We Access

When you authorize an integration, Home CFO may access data made available by that provider (for example: activity, sleep, recovery metrics). The exact data depends on the permissions you grant.

How We Use Data

  • To display metrics and insights inside Home CFO.
  • To improve your personal tracking and analytics.

Data Sharing

Home CFO does not sell personal data. Home CFO does not share your connected-provider data with third parties, except as required to operate the integration you explicitly enabled.

Data Storage

Access tokens and integration data are stored securely and are used only to fetch data you authorized. You can revoke access at any time from the provider’s settings and/or within Home CFO (where available).
